FAQ

Everything you need to know about AEO.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)? +
Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your digital presence so that AI-powered answer engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot — cite your brand as the authoritative answer to relevant queries. Unlike traditional SEO which targets search rankings, AEO targets AI-generated responses and recommendations.
How is AEO different from SEO? +
SEO targets rankings in search engine results pages. AEO targets appearances in AI-generated answers. AEO requires entity authority building, structured data, and semantic content that signals trustworthiness to AI training and retrieval systems — skills that most SEO agencies don't have yet.
Why do SaaS companies need AEO? +
SaaS buyers increasingly use AI to research and compare tools before purchasing. When a prospect asks ChatGPT "what's the best [category] tool," the AI's response shapes their decision. Companies optimized for AEO appear in these recommendations; those who ignore AEO are invisible to a fast-growing, high-intent buyer segment.
How long does AEO take to show results? +
Quick wins appear in 4–12 weeks in Google AI Overviews and Bing AI. Consistent citations in ChatGPT and Perplexity typically take 2–6 months. AeroAEO's entity-first approach accelerates this significantly compared to content-only approaches.
What does AeroAEO's process look like? +
Three phases: Audit (we map your AI visibility gaps and competitor positions), Optimize (schema markup, entity building, AI-citation-ready content), and Scale (ongoing citation signals through content, PR, and partnerships). Every engagement starts with a free audit.
Do I still need SEO if I do AEO? +
AEO and SEO are complementary. Strong AEO actually improves traditional SEO signals too — structured data, content quality, and entity authority benefit both. We build AEO strategies that reinforce your existing SEO foundation rather than replace it.
