AeroAEO built its own AEO Score Calculator to grade sites on a 10-point framework. Now we're opening it up as a certification: pass the audit, and you get a verified "AEO Certified" badge to embed on your own site — a credential and a backlink in one.

The 10 Certification Criteria

Each item is worth 10 points. A score of 80 or higher qualifies for certification. This is the same framework used in our free AEO Score Calculator — try it yourself before applying.

  • 1

    Schema Markup

    Organization and WebSite schema implemented and valid.

    10 pts
  • 2

    FAQ Schema

    FAQPage schema on at least one key page, matching visible Q&A content.

    10 pts
  • 3

    Entity / About Page & Consistent NAP

    A clear About/entity page, with name, description, and contact details matching everywhere else you appear online.

    10 pts
  • 4

    2,000+ Word Cornerstone Article

    One comprehensive, authoritative piece AI engines can cite as a primary source.

    10 pts
  • 5

    Structured Headings

    Clear H1–H3 hierarchy, phrased as the real questions your users ask.

    10 pts
  • 6

    Sitemap Submission

    A valid sitemap.xml, submitted to Google Search Console and/or Bing Webmaster Tools.

    10 pts
  • 7

    AI-Crawler-Friendly robots.txt

    robots.txt explicitly allows GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and other AI crawlers.

    10 pts
  • 8

    Glossary Page

    A definitional glossary of terms relevant to your industry — the kind of content AI engines cite constantly.

    10 pts
  • 9

    Case Study / Original Data

    At least one case study or piece of original data/research that other sites could cite.

    10 pts
  • 10

    Active Backlinks & Citations

    Evidence of third-party mentions, directory listings, or backlinks pointing to your site.

    10 pts
