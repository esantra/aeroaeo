FAQ

What is the AEO Certified Marketer program?

A paid, self-paced certification: an 8-module study guide plus a 60-question exam. Score 80% or higher (48/60) to earn your verified badge and certificate.

What if I don't pass on my first attempt?

Retake as many times as you need at no extra cost, with no cap. You'll see a module-by-module breakdown after every attempt so you know exactly what to review before your next try. Practically speaking, that means you cannot fail to eventually get certified — you just keep going.

Why does the price go up later?

The first 25 seats are priced at $199 to reward early adopters while the program is new. Once those 25 are claimed, the price moves to $299 for everyone after. Founding-cohort members keep their access forever regardless of future price changes.

How is this different from AeroAEO's free business certification?

The free AEO Certification audits a business's website against the 10-point framework. This program certifies a marketer's own knowledge and skills — it's a personal professional credential, not a site audit.

Is the exam timed?

No. Take as long as you need, and keep the study guide open in another tab while you work through it.
